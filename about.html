About

WeatherApp is a simple and user-friendly application designed to provide real-time weather updates for any city in the world. With just a quick search, you can instantly check the current conditions, hourly forecasts, and weekly predictions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make weather tracking simple, reliable, and accessible for everyone. Whether you are planning a trip, scheduling your day, or just curious about the forecast, WeatherApp ensures you get accurate and real-time weather information with ease.

Features

  • 🌍 Search weather for any city worldwide
  • ⏰ Hourly, daily, and weekly forecasts
  • ⭐ Save your favorite or recent locations
  • 🌡️ Switch between °C and °F
  • 🌙 Clean, responsive, and modern design

Technology Stack

WeatherApp is built with modern web technologies:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3 / Bootstrap
  • JavaScript
  • Weather API

Meet the Developer

WeatherApp is developed as a frontend project to practice and showcase web development skills. It combines design, responsive UI, and API integration to deliver a smooth and useful weather experience.
